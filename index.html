Forgotten Roast

Forgotten Roast is a personal favorite of mine. It's a fairly simple recipe capable of feeding many heads with about 30 minutes of prep time. You must plan in advance as the recipe takes at least 5 hours of cook time. It can be done in a crock pot, but for best results use your oven(It tastes way better).

Ingredients

  • Chuck Roast
  • Potatoes(baby red skin or russet peeled and cubed)
  • Carrots 2-3(cut in large chunks)
  • Onions 2-3(cut in large chunks)
  • 1 can of Cream of Mushroom soup
  • 1 package of dry onion soup mix
