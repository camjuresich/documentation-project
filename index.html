Forgotten Roast

Forgotten Roast is a rich meat dish with a creamy sauce. It's a fairly simple recipe capable of feeding many heads with about 30 minutes of prep time. You must plan in advance as the recipe takes at least 5 hours of cook time. It can be done in a crock pot, but for best results use your oven(It tastes way better).

Ingredients

  • Chuck Roast
  • Potatoes(baby red skin or russet peeled and cubed)
  • Carrots 2-3(cut in large chunks)
  • Onions 2-3(cut in large chunks)
  • 2 cans of Cream of Mushroom soup
  • 1 package of dry onion soup mix

Putting it all together

  • — Pre-heat oven to 325℉
  • — Grab an oven-safe dish large enough for all your ingredients.
  • — Line the dish with foil(use 2 pieces if needed).
  • — Dust onion soup mix on both sides of chuck roast.
  • — Add potatoes, onions, and carrots around meat.
  • — Dump cream of mushroom soup over top of meat.
  • — Place and crimp foil over top of dish
  • — bake for 4-5 hours or until meat pulls apart easily.
Cheesy Potato Soup

This soup will have you wanting more even if you don't have room for it. It's a cream base loaded with cheese, potatoes and bacon. This recipe will take about an hour of cook and prep time included. It pairs well with a garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • 6 to 8 large potatoes(peeled cubed and cooked)
  • 2 cups milk
  • 4 cups water
  • 4 chicken bouillion cubes(dissolved in water)
  • 2/3 cups flour
  • 2/3 cups butter
  • 8-12 bacon(cooked)
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 8oz shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • (optional) chives

Putting it all together

  • — In a large pot, combine butter and flour over low heat to form a roux
  • — Cook for one minute adding milk and water slowly
  • — add salt, pepper, bacon, and cheese
  • — when everything is well blended and cheese is melted add sour cream
  • — add more salt and pepper to taste if needed
  • — soup will thicken with age. Add milk or water when reheating
  • — serve with chives
Spicy Potato Soup

You want a soup that will warm you on a cold day? Look no further! Spicy potato soup is a tomato base soup that has a bit of a kick to it. It's fairly cheap to make and can be modified to suit your taste

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2-2 pounds ground beef
  • 3-4 onions chopped in large chunks
  • 6-8 pototoes peeled and cubed
  • 1 large can tomato sauce
  • 8 cups water
  • chili powder and/or whatever kine hot sauce you like

Putting it all together

  • — In a large pot, brown ground beef w/ onions and drain
  • — add water potatoes and tomato sauce
  • — bring to boil, reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes
  • — add seasoning, salt and pepper to taste
White Chicken Chili

A different take on chili, White chicken chili is easy to make and has a powerful and hearty flavor great for cold days or when you don't really want to cook.

Ingredients

  • 2 packages McCormick white chicken chili mix
  • 2 pounds chicken cooked and cubed
  • 4 cans white beans
  • 2 cups water
  • 1-2 green peppers(cut in chunks)

Putting it all together

  • — dump all your ingredients(except green pepper into soup and simmer for 25 minutes
  • — add your green peppers for the last 5 minutes and spice to taste
  • — can also be made all day in a crock pot
